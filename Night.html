The Nightmare Before Christmas

The Nightmare Before Christmas was made in 1993 as a stop-motion animated film. This film is a fantasy story based on the holidays of the year but with 2 main holidays - Halloween & Christmas. It all starts with the pumpkin king of Halloween being bored of yearly celebration. After escaping the halloween celebration, Jack- the pumpkin king, he wanders off and encounters 7 trees all themed with a holiday. Jack was amazed with what he saw but one tree caught his eye the most- Christmas. And his new adventure begins.
